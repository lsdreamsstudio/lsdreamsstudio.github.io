Hey... Welcome!

LSDreams is a solo game studio by Luiz Souza, where every project is a dive into the strange, the surreal, and the unexpected. The name blends LS (Luiz Souza) with a wink to LSD—because games, like dreams (or the occasional trip), should take you somewhere you've never been.

Based in a quiet beach town in Brazil, LSDreams focuses on roguelikes and other genre-bending experiences that mix strategy, emotion, and a bit of chaos. Whether it’s cursed crops or card-based battles under a blood moon, each game is designed to surprise, challenge, and linger in your mind long after you stop playing.

This blog is where you'll find devlogs, experiments, and behind-the-scenes thoughts from the solo journey of turning wild ideas into playable worlds.

Thanks for stepping into the dream!
